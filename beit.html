About BE.IT

The Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology program is designed to product high quality experts in the field of information technology. It is a four-year program spread over eight semesters. A student needs to successful complete of 133 credit hours of course work, practical and project work for graduation.

Download BE.IT full Syllabus:

Eligibility

Students should have scored minimum of "C" Grade in all subjects in 10+2 (Secondary Level)
OR
I.Sc. or 10+2 (Science), or I.E. or equivalent 2nd division or above from University or boards recognized by Pokhara University.
